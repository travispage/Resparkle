Overview

Approved Comment Notifier is a great way to keep your visitors connected with you. Once a visitor, or a member, of your site write a comment on your website, he'll get a custom email neotification once its comment is approved by a member of your administration. Simple and nice ! You can of course customize the email, include logo, colors and variables!

Installation

The installation process is very simple. Just like with any other Wordpress plugin you will have to follow through the steps below :

  1. Log in to your Wordpress administration panel
  2. Click Add New in the Plugins menu on the left side
  3. Click on the Upload link at the top of the page
  4. Locate and upload the plugin zip archive
  5. After the package is uploaded and extracted, Click Activate Plugin

After going through the points above, you should now see some new widgets under "Settings > Approved Comment Notifier" menu. If this is not the case, make sure you followed through the step above and repeat them as necessary.

Usage

To create a custom email simply go to "Settings > Approved Comment Notifier" and write your own email. You can use any of the following variables. This variables, written between brackets, will automatically be replaced by the corresponding value.

Here are the available variables:

  • comment_author
  • comment_date
  • commented_post_name
  • commented_post_url
  • site_name
  • site_url

Simply use any of these variables between brackets, example {comment_author}

Example

Here is the code i use for my site, you can start from it to create your own:

 

Dear {comment_author},

you wrote a comment on {site_name} on {comment_date} on the following post : {commented_post_name}. Guess what ? Your comment has just been approved, congratulations!

We would like to thank you very much for your interest and i hope to see you again very soon on my website.

Remi
{site_name}
