The ultimate Woocommerce plugin for Google AdWords advertising - embedding Conversion Tracking and Remarketing codes for you! 

== Description ==

Running a Google AdWords campaign?

2 things are crucial for you -

1. **Implementing Google AdWords conversion tracking** so you would know how effective your ads are; how many of the clicks you are paying for are actually gaining for you sales, installs or whatever you are trying to to reach.
2. **Implementing Google AdWords Remarketing Tag**, so that you can mark your site's visitors and show them more ads in the future until they are convinced to purchase your products/ services.

**This plugin will have both conversion tracking and remarketing codes embedded correctly on your site, so you can manage and optimize effective Google AdWords campaigns.**

If you have any questions/feedback, please send an email to support@storeya.com


== Installation ==

1. Install this 'Embed Google AdWords Codes on WooCommerce Plugin'.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Get relevant script from Google AdWords:

  a. see here how to get [Conversion Tracking Code](https://storeya.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/216545558-How-to-Generate-AdWords-Conversion-Tracking-Code)

  b. see here  how to get [Remarketing Code](https://storeya.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/216545448-How-to-tag-your-site-for-remarketing) 
4. In the WordpPress admin panel go to settings > Google AdWords Advertising menu. 
5. Enter each code to the relevant field, and click Save.
